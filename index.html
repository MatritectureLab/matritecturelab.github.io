Research Focus

Exploring the frontiers of computational architecture and matrix-based design methods

🔢

Matrix-Based Design

Developing high-dimensional matrix frameworks for generative architectural layout and structural optimization.

🤖

Machine Learning for Spaces

Applying deep learning and reinforcement learning to automate and augment spatial design workflows.

🏗️

Structural Computation

Integrating computational mechanics with data-driven approaches to achieve performative structural systems.

📊

Graph & Network Analysis

Representing buildings and urban systems as networks and graphs for topological and functional analysis.

🌐

Digital Fabrication

Bridging computational design with robotic fabrication, enabling direct design-to-manufacture pipelines.

🌱

Sustainable Systems

Using multi-objective optimization to balance structural performance, energy efficiency, and material use.
