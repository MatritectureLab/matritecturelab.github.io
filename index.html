MatriTecture Lab

We investigate the impact of the extracellular MATRIx archiTECTURE (MATRITECTURE) on cancer cell behavior, combining biochemical, biophysical, and functional approaches with big data modeling to understand metastasis and the tumor microenvironment.

Our Research Meet the Team

Research Focus

Exploring the dynamic architecture of the extracellular matrix

🧬

BM Architecture & Metastasis

Decoding how basement membrane (BM) stiffness and matrix architecture determine cancer cell invasion and metastasis formation.

🔬

RevMatriRegs

Investigating reversible ECM regulators, like Netrin-4, and their control over global extracellular matrix stiffness and cell behavior.

💡

Tumor Microenvironment

Exploring the communication between cancer and stromal cells, and deciphering signaling programs induced by released ECM factors.

💻

Big Data & Modeling

Combining biochemical, biophysical, and functional experimental approaches with big data modeling and mathematical simulations.

🧪

Translational Matrix Biology

Translating our fundamental findings on ECM protein activity into novel diagnostic and therapeutic applications for solid tumors.

🦠

ECM-Cell Interactions

Analyzing how cells interact with the ECM through cell adhesion complexes (CAMs) and integrin-mediated signaling pathways.
