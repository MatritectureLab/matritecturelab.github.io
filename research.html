Research

Exploring computational methods that bridge mathematics, data science, and spatial engineering.

Research Areas

Our work spans six core themes that intersect computation, mathematics, and design

🔢

Matrix-Based Architectural Design

We develop high-dimensional matrix representations of architectural layouts, enabling systematic exploration of design spaces through linear algebra and tensor decomposition techniques.

Tensor Decomposition Generative Design Layout Synthesis
🤖

Machine Learning for Spatial Design

We apply deep learning, graph neural networks, and reinforcement learning to automate and augment spatial design workflows, from room placement to urban-scale masterplanning.

Graph Neural Networks Reinforcement Learning Generative AI
🏗️

Structural Computation

Integrating computational mechanics, finite element methods, and data-driven surrogate models to achieve performative structural systems with minimal material use.

FEM Surrogate Models Topology Optimization
📊

Graph & Network Analysis

Buildings and cities are represented as graphs, enabling topological, functional, and accessibility analysis. We develop new metrics and algorithms tailored to architectural graphs.

Graph Theory Network Analysis Space Syntax
🌐

Digital Fabrication

We bridge computational design with robotic fabrication processes, developing algorithms for toolpath generation, material-aware design, and direct design-to-manufacture pipelines.

Robotic Fabrication Toolpath Planning Material Computing
🌱

Sustainable Systems Design

Multi-objective optimization approaches that simultaneously balance structural performance, energy efficiency, material use, and occupant comfort across building and urban scales.

Multi-Objective Opt. Energy Simulation LCA

Active Projects

Ongoing funded research initiatives

2024 – 2027

MatrixFloor: Tensor-Based Floor Plan Generation

A three-year project developing tensor factorization methods for automated floor plan synthesis that respects architectural constraints and user preferences.

2023 – 2026

GraphArch: Graph Learning for Building Analysis

Using graph neural networks to learn structural and spatial patterns from building datasets, enabling automatic code compliance checking and design recommendation.

2025 – 2028

RoboFab: Adaptive Robotic Fabrication

Developing real-time feedback loops between robotic fabrication systems and computational design models, enabling adaptive manufacturing of complex architectural geometries.

2024 – 2026

SustainOpt: Multi-Scale Sustainability Optimization

Integrating building energy simulation with structural optimization to co-optimize performance across environmental, structural, and functional dimensions.

2025 – 2027

UrbanMatrix: City-Scale Design Analysis

Extending matrix and graph methods to the urban scale, analyzing block configurations, street networks, and land use patterns using large-scale spatial datasets.

2023 – 2025

StructLearn: ML Surrogate Models for Structures

Training neural network surrogate models that approximate finite element analysis, reducing computational cost in iterative structural optimization workflows.
